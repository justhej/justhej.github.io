2024-01-02
0 minute read

This was a somewhat tedious chapter because the puzzles didn’t allow for more solutions than one or two at most.

Still, I tried to experiment as much as was possible. For instance, tuple-based conditional switch:

1moveForward()
2switch (isOnClosedSwitch, isOnGem) {
3case (true, _):
4    toggleSwitch()
5case (_, true):
6    collectGem()
7default:
8    print("No switch or gem found.")

Which is something I had seen in C# and wanted to try out in Swift.
